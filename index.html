Development of Bangladesh

In a last few years Bangadesh has being developing a lot. A lot of projects have been done in last 3 years. By completing these projects Bangladesh is developed a lot.

Megaprojects of Bangladesh

Here the name of some projects done in Bangladesh

  1. Metro Rail
  2. Padma bridge
  3. Karaphuli tunnel
  4. 300 ft highway
  5. 3rd terminal in HAZRAT SHAHJALAL INTERNOATIONAL AIRPORT
  6. Dhaka elevated expressway
  7. Cox's bazar rail station

As we can see that here all the projects open in Bangladesh are related to transport sector, so a question can raise in your mind that why all the project are related to transport sector? It is because in a populated country like Bangladesh we must need better transport system, so for this reason Bangladesh government devloped many projects related to transportation.
